Privacy Policy and Terms Of Service




Glagotias Attendance Checker (https://gu-attendance.darshan.ninja) is a student-run "unofficial" online webapp for the students of Galgotias University. This webapp is intended to be used to recieve a daily automated notification of their attendance status. The Data entered by students is highly critical and the following privacy policy defines how we use this.




What information we collect


For the optimal working of the application, Galgotias Attendance Checker collects your college Admission Number and the password that is used to "login" into rfCampus. These two are the minimal requirement for the functioning of the app. Apart from this we also ask for an email - which can be any email and is not verified for your privacy. You can absolutely go ahead and fill "NA" in the email field. We do not store any local cookies on your device other than Google Analytics. & Cloudflare. cookies.



How do we use the information


Your password and admission number are mission critical resources whenever the scheduler for notification runs (Generally at 07:00pm IST). We use this to make API Request to rfCampus. Three types of API calls are made by the app before sending a notification. These include 'Login Call', 'Call for Subject List', 'Call(s) for Individual Subject Attendance'. UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCE WE DO NOT MAKE ANY OTHER API CALL. The sole purpose of this app is attendance staus and it makes only those required calls.
Emails collected maybe used to send occasional mails and updates about my projects. No information collected is shared/sold under any circumstances.



How do we store your information


We understand your data is highly crucial. That's why this Privacy Policy exist. This is how your data is stored and fetched at various stages:
1. The complete website is end to end secured with HTTPS connection by Cloudflare and Firebase Hosting.
2. The password is not sent via a "normal" https call. Firebase SDK's HTTPS onCall functions are used to ensure the highest possible level of data protection by the app.
3. We maintain multiple database for the app. Your data is stored in noSQL database which has all the HTTPS/HTTP ports closed. This means the database, is not accessible via the internet. It is only accessible via the server application within Google Cloud's infrastructure.
4. The client side database does not store any crucial information.
5. All the databases are encrypted at server side.
6. During loging, it is ensured that only your unique (server-generated) user id is logged.
